Welcome to the ACM family! You are officially part of us now. ACM is Santa Clara's premiere organization for computer science and computer engineering. Our goal for our members is to develop real-world CS skills and experience, to promote professional development through jobs and internships, and to cultivate a tight-knit, collaborative community. And of course, we will be having lots of fun along the way through different weekly workshops and events. We are thrilled that you can join us! :) 

Please feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions at acm@scu.edu

Follow ACM's Google Calendar 

Here are three ways you can keep up with the ACM community:
 The newsletter you just signed up for
This newsletter is sent out every Monday morning with tons of info! That includes upcoming events for the week, sign up forms, hackathon event dates, and even job/internship opportunities. Make sure to read it every week so you don't miss out on anything!

 ACM Discord
Join our Discord server to stay up to date with events and chat with other ACMers! From gaming to just hanging out, Discord has it all. We recently remade it, so there will be plenty of opportunities to hear the latest ACM news or get help with your classes.

 ACM Instagram (@scu_acm)
We post to our Instagram daily, from reminders about our events the day they're happening to live updates on what we're up to, Instagram is the best way to connect with ACM on a day-to-day basis. Make sure to drop us a follow and check our stories often!
Join the #scuacm Discord channel. Enough said.
